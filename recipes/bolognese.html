Description

This is a basic Spaghetti Bolognese recipe.

You can use any pasta for this recipe, but I find spaghetti and Fettuccine work best.

Ingredients

  • 1 x Tin of diced Tomatoes
  • 1 Tablespoon of Tomato paste
  • 500g Lean beef mince
  • 1 x Onion
  • 2 x Garlic cloves
  • 1/2 packet of Spaghetti pasta
  • 1 Tablespoon of Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Parmesan Cheese to serve

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta Al Dente according to packet directions and drain setting 1/2 cup some of the water
  2. Dice the Onion and Garlic
  3. While the pasta is cooking, in a saucepan on a medium heat, add the oil and fry the Onion until translucent adding a pinch of salt to draw out the moisture
  4. Add the garlic and fry for a further 30 seconds
  5. Add the beef mince and with a wooden spoon, break the mince down and fry until it browned
  6. Add the tomato paste and fry for another 30 seconds
  7. Add the tin of Tomatoes and pasta water saved from step one
  8. Add Salt and Pepper to taste, and simmer for 10 minutes
  9. Serve on a plate with sprinkle of Parmesan Cheese
